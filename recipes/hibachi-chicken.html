Perfect Hibachi Chicken

Why You'll Love This Recipe

  • Perfect copycat: It tastes just like the Benihana version, without the trip to the restaurant.
  • No equipment required: You don't need a hibachi grill - just make everything in a skillet!
  • Healthier: You're using homemade ingredients so you can control the calories and nutrition,
  • Cheaper: It's EXPENSIVE to go to Benihana! Making it at home is cost-effective.

Ingredients

Hibachi Chicken

  • Chicken breasts
  • Sesame oil
  • Soy sauce
  • Hoisin sauce
  • Garlic
  • Ginger

Mustard sauce

  • Light mayo
  • Soy sauce
  • Rice vinegar
  • Dijon Mustard

Sauteed vegetables

  • Olive oil
  • Carrots, mushrooms, onion, and zucchini
  • Soy sauce

Fried Rice

  • Cooked white rice
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Soy sauce
  • Egg
  • Green onions

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken
  2. Cook the chicken
  3. Make the mustard sauce
  4. Saute the veggies
  5. Make the fried rice
  6. Assemble your dish
