Best Homemade Pizza

Description

I love this Easy Homemade Pizza Recipe! I have been perfecting this recipe for years. It is quick to put together and so versatile. I'll give you lots of tips, tricks, and ideas to get the best homemade pizza to ever come out of your wimpy, not-wood-fired oven!

Equipment

  • Pizza Stone
  • Parchment Paper
  • Pizza Peel or Flat Baking Sheet

Ingredients

  • 1 16 ounce ball of pizza dough
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 3 ounces thinly sliced mozzarella cheese
  • 5 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2.5 ounces pepperoni
  • 2 tablespoons shredded parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Place a pizza stone in the bottom third of your oven
  2. Preheat your oven to 550 degrees F
  3. Prepare a work surface
  4. Roll out the dough
  5. Transfer the dough to a square of parchment paper
  6. Par bake
  7. Top your pizza
  8. Bake the pizza for 8-12 minutes
  9. Remove from oven and enjoy!
